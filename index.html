plaintext.gg

why plaintext?

plaintext.gg is a distraction-free writing tool.

no formatting. no folders. no sync conflicts.

just open the page and start typing.

your text is saved locally in your browser. nothing is sent to any server. ever.

it is the simplest way to write.

privacy

plaintext.gg stores everything in your browser's localStorage.

no cookies. no analytics. no tracking. no accounts. no server. no database.

your text never leaves your device.

the entire site is static HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and fonts.

you can verify this yourself — view source.

we believe the best privacy policy is not needing one at all.
