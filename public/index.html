about

plaintext.gg is a simple text editor.

no ai. no formatting. no cookies. no analytics. no tracking. no accounts.

your text is saved locally in your browser. nothing is sent to any server. ever.

thank you Commit Mono (opens in new tab). thank you OpenDyslexic (opens in new tab). thank you Phosphor (opens in new tab).

self hostable and open sourced at GitHub (opens in new tab).
