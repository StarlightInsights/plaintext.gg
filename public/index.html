why plaintext.gg?

plaintext.gg is a distraction-free writing tool.

no ai. no formatting.

just open the page and start typing.

your text is saved locally in your browser. nothing is sent to any server. ever.

just a simple way to write, take notes, and strip formatting.

open-source.

privacy

plaintext.gg stores text in your browser's IndexedDB.

theme, toolbar visibility, and font size stay in your browser's localStorage.

no cookies. no analytics. no tracking. no accounts.

hosted on bunny.net

your text never leaves your device.

fonts and icons are bundled with the site.

we believe the best privacy policy is not needing one at all.
